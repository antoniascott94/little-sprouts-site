About Us

Welcome to Little Sprouts Learning Center! We are a nurturing and inclusive early childhood education program dedicated to fostering the growth and development of young children. Our experienced educators create a safe, engaging, and stimulating environment where children can explore, learn, and thrive.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide a high-quality, developmentally appropriate early childhood education that supports the whole child—socially, emotionally, physically, and cognitively—while building strong partnerships with families and the community.

Program Information by Age Group

Infants (6 weeks – 12 months): A nurturing environment focused on sensory exploration and bonding. Toddlers (1 – 2 years): Hands-on activities that promote language, motor skills, and independence. Preschool (3 – 5 years): A structured curriculum that prepares children for kindergarten through play-based learning.
